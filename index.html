ERAS

A Knowledge Extraction and Representation project inspired by Taylor Swift's ERAS tour.

Get Started

The project

ERAS is a project that aims to represent Taylor’s Swift music production through knowledge extraction and representation.
From her beginnings as a teenage country sensation to her status as a Time’s Person of the year, becoming a billionaire only with her own music, she has demonstrated her strong attitude and capacity of reinventing herself, through showing her songwriting and storytelling skills among the eleven-studio album.
In March 2023 she embarked the Eras Tour, a retrospective tour covering all her albums. The tour has got a massive cultural and economic impact broking the record for the highest-grossing tour in history.
The show delineates the diverse array of eras that define the artist’s musical journey, from the raw authenticity of her early country roots to the pop anthems of the 1989 era and the introspective depth of her more mature albums.
The main research question is to understand how her production evolves through time from a semantic point of view, by extracting the central themes embedded within her lyrics, exploring the various genres as well.

DICSCLAIMER: music is subjective! The topic modeling in this project is not extremely accurate, but It’s interesting to see how the machine interprets music though it, making a comparison with our perspective.

Workflow

The workflow of the project is divided mainly in four steps:

1. Formulation of research and competency questions:


As already explained, the general purpose is for the project to reflect all the 11 eras in terms of topics to then satisfy some curiosities about their evolution.

The main research question is:

How do the topics and themes in Taylor Swift's lyrics evolve across her discography, and what relationships exist between these topics, the genres, and the changes in her production and label affiliations?

This main question encapsulates detailed inquires, indeed, the query part will be accomplished in the last step of the project, which will give objective conclusions to more precise questions.

2. Knowledge Extraction:


The second step has been the extraction of the main topics though topic modeling. In the first place, comprehensive datasets have been gathered from this source , which allowed to work on well-organized data, containing all the lyrics for each album.

3. Ontology development:


The third step involves the development of the ontology using Protégé.

4. SPARQL queries:


The last sep concerns the exploitation of the knowledge graph, derived from the ontology, through SPARQL queries. The competency questions in natural language have been converted in SPARQL syntax in order to query the knowledge graph and obtain answers.
